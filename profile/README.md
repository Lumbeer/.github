# Lumbeer

**Construction & civil-engineering AI tools.**

We turn the documents that run construction projects — drawings, schedules, and
plans — into structured, reliable data, so engineering teams stop transcribing by
hand and start trusting what's on the page.

## Problems we're solving

- **Manual takeoffs are slow and error-prone.** Pulling columns, walls, dimensions,
  and notes off drawings by hand burns engineering hours and invites mistakes.
- **Critical data is locked inside drawings.** Information trapped in PDFs can't be
  searched, checked, or reused anywhere downstream.
- **Quality review doesn't scale.** Spotting missing elements and wrong dimensions
  across hundreds of sheets is tedious, inconsistent, and easy to skip.

## What we've already solved

- **Structural drawing extraction** — columns, walls, dimensions, and notes pulled
  straight from drawings into clean, structured data.
- **Takeoff automation** — plans and schedules turned into reviewable, reusable
  outputs instead of manual spreadsheets.
- **Built-in quality checks** — missing, duplicated, and questionable items flagged
  before they ever reach design.
